Information about the System

— What is the proyect about? —

The project consists of the development of online voting software that would allow voting from a device connected to the Internet, it can be an internal network and controlled by the institution that organizes the elections. In this way, the voter should not move to the polling place and will be able to cast their vote through the network.

— What is the idea? —

Between the work team we formulated several innovative programs that would be of great interest to the community, among them was the Voting System formulated by one of its creators, Gabriela Barrera, who proposed an electronic voting system, since currently the country does not have one and it would be of great interest for the population to be able to vote in an electronic way that would facilitate the user and their form of the right to vote.

— Function —

The program verifies the entry of voter data, requiring personal data and checking if they are suitable to vote, allow the selection of one of the options presented, and verify if it is the correct choice, through this vote data graphs will be updated , on the number of votes presented by each selection and on the votes of each department.

Frequent questions

What should I do if the installation doesn't start?

It is probably that we have disabled the program because we are doing maintenance or it may be a connection problem.

What should I do if the system does not start after installation?

Verify that your operating system is compatible with the requirements that the program asks for. If it is not the operating system of your device, it may be an error in the frameworks installed, update them to the required version. If it is none, please contact us!

What should I do if I can´t remember the password?

Don't worry, the system can send you a new password to the linked email and if in case you lost the email, you should contact us and we will establish where to send you a verification password to a new email.
